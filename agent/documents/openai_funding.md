# OpenAI Funding Information

## Latest Funding Rounds

### Series F (March 2025)
In March 2025, OpenAI completed a Series F funding round raising $40 billion, valuing the company at $300 billion post-money. This round was led by SoftBank Group, with participation from Microsoft, Coatue, Altimeter, and Thrive.

### Series E (October 2024)
In October 2024, OpenAI raised $6.6 billion at a $157 billion valuation. Investors included Microsoft, Nvidia, SoftBank, Thrive Capital, Khosla Ventures, and Fidelity. This marked Nvidia's first investment in OpenAI.

## Financial Overview
As of late 2025, OpenAI's valuation had reached approximately $500 billion. The company's annual recurring revenue exceeded $13 billion by August 2025 and was projected to pass $20 billion by the end of 2025.
